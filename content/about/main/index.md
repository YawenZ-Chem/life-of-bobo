  Bald Head, also known as Bobo, is a Sphynx cat living in Boston with his humans. He may look like a tiny scary creature from another planet, but in reality, he is a professional cuddle lover who wants human warmth more than anything.

  Bobo was born in Russia on June 26, 2021. During Thanksgiving 2021, he traveled from Russia to New York City and then to Boston, where he officially began his career as a bald Boston resident.

He has also built an impressive career in outfit modeling, party hosting, birthday celebrations, and household supervision.
